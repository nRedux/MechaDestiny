Demo scene requires Postprocessing 2.3 or higher and High Definision RP 7.18 or higher (for HDRP scene)
The project contains Standard Materials that you can change to HDRP at any time by unpacking and importing the package: HDRP/HDRP_Settings_and_Materials. 
In addition, the project contains copies of Standard Materials - HDRP / Backup_Standard_Materials
